Colour Theme: 
  The website will have a black, grey, and dark blue colour palette (#000000 #14213D #E5E5E5) for a sleek and technological feel. The monochromatic black and grey will give a feel of silver and metal, highlighting the modern high-tech design of the IsoGen I.

Typography: 
  There are three fonts used:
    Display: Semi-Straight Regular Style Capitol City Font (https://www.fontspace.com/capitol-city-font-f145949)
    Headline: Impact Font (https://www.dafontfree.io/impact-font/)
    Paragraph: Kefa Regular (https://fonnts.com/kefa/)

Website Design Extra:
  Buttons and designs have hard edges, no rounded corners. Simplistic website.
